üöÄ Getting Started

Welcome to the Sinhala Sign Language LMS! Follow these steps to begin your learning journey:

  1. 1
    Enable Camera Access
    When prompted, allow the browser to access your webcam. This is required for sign recognition. Your privacy is protected - all processing happens locally on your device.
  2. 2
    Choose Your Learning Mode
    Start with Practice Mode to learn signs with guided exercises, or take an Exam to test your current knowledge.
  3. 3
    Follow the Instructions
    Watch the demonstration and perform the sign shown. The AI will provide real-time feedback on your accuracy.
  4. 4
    Track Your Progress
    Visit the Dashboard to monitor your learning progress, view achievements, and access detailed analytics.
üí° Pro Tip
Ensure good lighting and position your hands clearly in the camera view for best recognition accuracy.

üéØ Practice Mode

Practice Mode helps you learn Sinhala signs through guided exercises and real-time feedback.

How Practice Mode Works:

  1. 1
    Select a Letter
    Choose a Sinhala letter from the progress grid to practice. Completed letters are marked with a green checkmark.
  2. 2
    Watch the Demonstration
    Study the sign demonstration image and the large letter display. Pay attention to hand position and movement.
  3. 3
    Perform the Sign
    Position your hands in the camera view and perform the sign. The AI will analyze your movement and provide feedback.
  4. 4
    Complete 3 Successful Attempts
    You need 3 correct attempts to master each letter. The progress dots show your current attempt count.
üí° Practice Tips
  • Keep your hands steady and visible in the camera
  • Ensure good lighting on your hands
  • Hold the sign position for 2-3 seconds
  • Use the confidence meter to gauge accuracy

üìù Exam Mode

Exam Mode tests your knowledge with timed assessments and multiple-choice questions.

Exam Features:

  • Timed Assessment: Complete the exam within the time limit
  • Multiple Choice: Choose the correct answer from 4 options
  • Real-time Feedback: See immediate results for each question
  • Detailed Results: Review your performance and accuracy
‚ö†Ô∏è Important

üîß Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions:

Camera not working or not detected ‚ñº
  • Check if your camera is connected and not being used by another application
  • Ensure you've granted camera permissions to your browser
  • Try refreshing the page and allowing camera access again
  • Check your browser's privacy settings
Sign recognition is not accurate ‚ñº
  • Ensure good lighting on your hands
  • Keep your hands clearly visible in the camera frame
  • Hold the sign position steady for 2-3 seconds
  • Make sure your hands are not overlapping or partially hidden
  • Try adjusting your camera angle or distance
Page loading slowly or not responding ‚ñº
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Close other tabs or applications using bandwidth
  • Try using a different browser
Progress not saving ‚ñº
  • Ensure you're not using private/incognito browsing mode
  • Check if your browser allows local storage
  • Try refreshing the page and continuing your session
  • Make sure you're completing the required number of attempts

‚ôø Accessibility Features

Our LMS is designed to be accessible to users with different abilities and needs.

  • Screen Reader Support: Compatible with screen reading software

  • Focus Management: Clear focus indicators and logical tab order
üí° Accessibility Tips
